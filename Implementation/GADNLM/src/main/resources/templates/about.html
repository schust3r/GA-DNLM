GA-DNLM

GA-DNLM stands for Genetic Algorithms and Deceived Non Local Means (using Fast Fourier Transforms), as proposed by professor Saúl Calderón on his original paper.

This implementation of Genetic Algorithms attempts to simplify the use and improve the results obtained from DNLM-IFFT Filter by providing a better calibration than if manually adjusted.

This website has been developed by ITCR students Joel Schuster and Joel Barrantes under the guidance of professor Saúl Calderón and PARMA-Group.
